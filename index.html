About The Owner

Ryan Lusby has always had a passion for working outside. Whether it was mowing his lawn or doing other yard chores, he always enjoyed the fresh air and the gratification of seeing a project go from start to finish. Ryan opened Lusby Lawncare in 2020 during the height of the pandemic and has been providing great service to The Villages and surrounding areas in Florida ever since!
